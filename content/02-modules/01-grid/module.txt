Text: The grid we use is a bit different that most traditional grids. Instead of pre-defining measurements, we simply created a consistent set of class names that allow the designer to customize the column sizing across the board.
----
Snippet:
<div class="grp grp-four"> <!-- Expects four ".col-" divs. -->
    <div class="col col-one">
        <!-- Column One -->
    </div>
    <div class="col col-two">
        <!-- Column Two -->
    </div>
    <div class="col col-three">
        <!-- Column Three -->
    </div>
    <div class="col col-four">
        <!-- Column Four -->
    </div>
</div>
